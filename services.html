Our Services

Reliable care when you need it the most.

Emergency Ambulance Services

  • 24×7 ambulance booking
  • Quick response under 10 minutes*
  • GPS-based location tracking
  • Certified EMTs for medical support
  • Basic Life Support (BLS) kits onboard
  • Hospital coordination & priority admission

Blood Donation Support

RESQuE maintains a verified donor network to help patients needing urgent blood. You can become a donor, search donors, or request help through our platform.

  • Register as a blood donor
  • Search available donors by blood group
  • Maintain a reliable community blood network
  • Instant phone contact with donors

Volunteer Support

Community volunteers play a huge role in life-saving situations. RESQuE enables:

  • First-aid helpers
  • CPR-certified volunteers
  • Community responders
  • On-site crowd control support

Medical Assistance

  • Basic first-aid guidance
  • Over-phone emergency support
  • Rapid trauma response
  • Transport to nearest equipped hospital

*Response time depends on traffic and ambulance availability.
